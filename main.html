Lentil and Potato Curry

You will need:

250g cooked puy lentils

6 shallots

4 garlic cloves

1.5 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp turmeric, cinnamon, paprika, coriander

1 red pepper

2 red chillies

200g chopped tomatoes

600g potatoes/other vegetable

100g spinach

400ml coconut milk

Juice of 1 lime (2 tbsps)

1 tbsp honey

3 tbsp soy sauce
