Lentil and Potato Curry

You will need:

250g cooked puy lentils

6 shallots

4 garlic cloves

1.5 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp turmeric, cinnamon, paprika, coriander

1 red pepper

2 red chillies

200g chopped tomatoes

600g potato/ veg

100g spinach

400ml coconut milk

Juice 1 lime (2 tbsps)

1 tbsp honey

3 tbsp soy sauce

What to do:

Cook potato/ other veg

Fry shallots, add spices, pepper, chillies

Add tomatoes 

Add honey, coconut milk, lime juice, soy sauce

Add cooked lentils, potato/ veg

Stir in spinach

Make it vegan!

Simply omit the honey
