Red Lentil, Chickpea and Chilli Soup

You will need:

2 tsp cuman

1 tsp chilli flakes

1 red onion

140g red split lentils

2 veg stock cubes and 2l water

400g can chopped tomato

200g chickpeas, rinsed and drained

What to do:

Dry fry cuman and chilli until aromas are released

Add onion and cook for 5 mins

Stir in lentils tomato and stock and bring to the boil

Reduce heat and simmer for 15 mins until lentils are soft

Blend and then stir through chickpeas and allow to heat for 5 mins

Lentil and Potato Curry

You will need:

250g cooked puy lentils

6 shallots

4 garlic cloves

1.5 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp turmeric, cinnamon, paprika, coriander

1 red pepper

2 red chillies

200g chopped tomatoes

600g potato/ veg

100g spinach

400ml coconut milk

Juice 1 lime (2 tbsps)

1 tbsp honey

3 tbsp soy sauce

What to do:

Cook potato/ other veg

Fry shallots, add spices, pepper, chillies

Add tomatoes 

Add honey, coconut milk, lime juice, soy sauce

Add cooked lentils, potato/ veg

Stir in spinach

Make it vegan!

Simply omit the honey
